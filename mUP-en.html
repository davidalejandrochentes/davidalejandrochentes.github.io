Conceived as a response to maintenance management challenges in the industrial sector, mUP was developed in close collaboration with Innovaplast Ecuador to optimize their operational processes. The project emerged from the need to create a comprehensive solution that enables efficient management of machinery, equipment, and resources.

Developed with a robust technological stack including Python, Django, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and PostgreSQL, mUP implements event-driven programming techniques to create a real-time notification system. The solution stands out for its modular architecture that allows organizing data by categories such as areas, machines, vehicles, computing equipment, and tools, including a specialized module for warehouse inventory management.

The system offers advanced features like personalized alerts configurable by days, hours, or kilometers, adapting to the specific needs of each module. Maintenance is recorded in detail, differentiating between preventive and corrective actions, with the ability to include visual documentation of the "before" and "after" states.

A distinctive feature of mUP is its ability to generate flexible reports, allowing the download of records in Excel format with advanced filtering options. This facilitates decision-making and detailed analysis of maintenance processes.

Finally, mUP implements a robust authentication system that ensures the security and confidentiality of information, restricting access only to authorized personnel. The project demonstrates the ability to create software solutions that optimize business processes through intelligent automation.
