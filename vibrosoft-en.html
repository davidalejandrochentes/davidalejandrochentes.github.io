Vibrosoft emerges in response to the scarcity of industrial monitoring and diagnostic systems in Cuba. Its main objective is to manage information obtained by data acquisition modules from anywhere in the world. This product interacts with physical hardware through bidirectional communication using the HTTP protocol.

This work was developed using the technologies (Python, Django, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, PostgreSQL).

It is important to highlight that this work was selected and presented at the International Scientific Convention of Engineering and Architecture, held at the Palacio de las Convenciones in Havana on November 28, 2024.
