This system was adopted by Innovaplast Ecuador to manage and organize maintenance of their machinery and equipment.

The technologies adopted for this work were (Python, Django, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, PostgreSQL).

mUP was designed and developed with the purpose of optimizing maintenance management within a company. It includes modules that allow organizing data by categories such as areas, machines, vehicles, computing equipment, and tools. Additionally, it has a special module to manage warehouse stock resources.

The system offers personalized alerts to notify the user about the opportune moment to perform maintenance. These alerts can be configured by days, hours, or kilometers, depending on the specific needs of each module.

Maintenance is registered as preventive or corrective, and in the case of vehicles, other relevant factors can also be included. Each maintenance record includes "before" and "after" images, allowing for visual tracking of the work performed.

The system facilitates downloading records in Excel format, also allowing filtering of maintenance for more specific data extraction.

Finally, mUP incorporates an authentication system to restrict access only to authorized personnel, ensuring the security and confidentiality of the information.
